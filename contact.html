Frequently Asked Questions

Are these chocolates truly organic and vegan?

Yes, all our chocolates are organic, vegan, non-GMO home-made dark chocolate with 75% purity. We carefully source our ingredients to ensure the highest quality and ethical standards.

How do the gift cards work?

Every 3 packages you purchase equals $15, which funds one restaurant gift card. These gift cards are distributed directly to homeless, impoverished, and less fortunate individuals in our community, allowing them to enjoy a meal at a local restaurant.

Do you ship?

Currently, we only sell in-person at various locations and events. We do not ship or sell through retail stores at this time. Contact us to find out where you can purchase our chocolate.

Is this a legitimate non-profit?

Yes, Golden Ticket Chocolates is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization with federal, state, and county licensing. We operate with full transparency, and 100% of our profits go directly to supporting our community through restaurant gift cards.

Why is there a limit of 3 packages per person?

As a small, home-made operation, we produce limited quantities of chocolate. The 3-package limit helps ensure that more community members have the opportunity to enjoy our chocolate and support our mission.

Can I make a donation without purchasing chocolate?

We appreciate your generosity! Please contact us directly to discuss donation options. We're always grateful for community support in any form.
