Frequently Asked Questions

Are these chocolates truly organic and vegan?

Yes, all the chocolates are organic, vegan, gluten-free, non-GMO home-made dark chocolate with 75% purity. I carefully source the ingredients to ensure the highest quality and ethical standards.

How do the gift cards work?

Every 3 packages you purchase equals $15, which funds one restaurant gift card. These gift cards go directly to neighbors facing hard times, giving them the chance to enjoy a warm meal at a local restaurant.

Do you ship?

Currently, I only sell in-person at various locations and events. I do not ship or sell through retail stores at this time. Reach out to find out where you can purchase my chocolate.

Is this a legitimate non-profit?

Yes, Golden Ticket Chocolates is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization with federal, state, and county licensing. I operate with full transparency, and 100% of profits go directly to supporting our community through restaurant gift cards.

Why is there a limit of 3 packages per person?

As a small, home-made operation, I produce limited quantities of chocolate. The 3-package limit helps ensure that more community members have the opportunity to enjoy the chocolate and support the mission.

Can I make a donation without purchasing chocolate?

I appreciate your generosity! Please reach out directly to discuss donation options. I'm always grateful for community support in any form.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept Cash, Credit, and Debit cards for all in-person purchases. This makes it easy and convenient to support the mission however you prefer to pay.
