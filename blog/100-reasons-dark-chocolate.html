Heart & Cardiovascular Health

  1. Combats the presence of free radicals, which cause chronic illness and disease.
  2. Improves the function of the vascular endothelium (the cells that line the insides of your blood vessels).
  3. Reduces the risk of cardiovascular disease.
  4. Raises HDL (good cholesterol) and protects LDL from oxidation (bad cholesterol).
  5. Improves blood flow and lowers blood pressure.
  6. Sends good cholesterol up and bad cholesterol down.
  7. Can increase the production of nitric oxide, which helps relax blood vessels and improve blood flow.
  8. Contains an epicatechin compound which may help lower blood pressure and enhance endothelial functions.
  9. May improve endothelial function and insulin sensitivity in hypertensive individuals.

Antioxidants & Disease Prevention

  1. Powerful source of antioxidants due to flavonoids.
  2. Contains compounds that help combat oxidative stress and inflammation in the body, potentially lowering the risk of chronic diseases such as arthritis and type 2 diabetes.
  3. Plays a role in cancer prevention by fighting free radicals.
  4. Associated with a lower risk of cancer mortality.

Brain Function & Mental Health

  1. Improves brain function.
  2. Improves cognition, prevents memory loss, and boosts your mood.
  3. Reduces stress.
  4. Enhances mood from polyphenolic compounds.
  5. Contains serotonin precursors, which can help elevate mood and alleviate symptoms of depression and anxiety.
  6. May improve brain function and help prevent neurodegenerative conditions, such as Alzheimer's disease and Parkinson's disease.
  7. Flavonoids present may enhance neuroplasticity, which is the brain's ability to reorganize itself, particularly in response to injury and disease.
  8. Increases the diversity of the microbiome, which can affect mood through the gut-brain axis.

Skin & Beauty

  1. Protects your skin from the sun.
  2. Supports production of collagen, keeping skin looking healthy and young.
  3. Increases skin density and hydration, and contributes to a healthier and more radiant complexion.

Weight Management & Digestion

  1. Good for your gut and helps with weight loss.
  2. High in fiber.
  3. Can promote feelings of satiety, reduce cravings for sweet, salty, and fatty foods, and consequently aid in reducing overall calorie intake.
  4. High fiber content and presence of bioactive compounds can help in managing weight.

Diabetes & Blood Sugar

  1. Improves blood sugar levels and reduces the risk of developing diabetes.

Physical Performance

  1. Enhances men's physical performance due to its ability to increase nitric oxide levels in the body, improving blood flow and reducing oxygen consumption during physical activities.
  2. Improves endurance and reduces fatigue during exercise.

Dental Health

  1. Contains polyphenols that can help prevent tooth decay.
  2. Antibacterial properties that can reduce plaque formation.
  3. Has a theobromine compound, which might help harden the tooth enamel.

Respiratory Health

  1. Theobromine may suppress the function of the vagus nerve (a nerve that causes coughing) and may help reduce coughs.

Vision

  1. Boosts vision.

Essential Nutrients

  1. Packed with important minerals such as iron, magnesium, zinc, copper, and phosphorus.
  2. Largely vegan (cocoa is vegan in its natural form).
