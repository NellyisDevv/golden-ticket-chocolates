How Your Purchase Makes a Difference

Step 1: You Purchase

When you buy our premium dark chocolate, 100% of the profits go directly toward purchasing restaurant gift cards.

Every 3 packages ($15) equals one gift card for a neighbor who could use a warm meal.

Step 2: I Partner

I partner with local restaurants to purchase gift cards. This supports neighborhood businesses while preparing to help folks in our community.

Step 3: Direct Distribution

Gift cards go directly to neighbors facing hard times right here in our community. No middlemen, no bureaucracy—just neighbors helping neighbors.
